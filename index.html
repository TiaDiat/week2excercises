My Favorite Super Heroes

Wonder Woman

She is an immortal Amazon warrior goddess, also known as Princess Diana. As the daughter of Queen Hippolyta and Zeus and as half-sister of Ares, she was given to the Amazons so that they could raise her. She is the most famous heroine of all time. She is a skilled fighter and her specialty is using weapons i.e. sword or whip.

Superman

Superman is the most powerful being on planet Earth, an alien immigrant named Kal-El from the planet Krypton who was raised in Smallville, Kansas. He was adopted by a couple who raised him on their farm. They named him Clark Kent and inspired him to become a hero. He has the ability to fly and has unbelivable strength and speed.

Iron Man

Anthony Edward "Tony" Stark is an engineering superhero! Although Tony Stark is a human and doesn't have any super powers, he is an incredible engineer. He created the Iron Man suit of armor that allows him to have superhuman strength, use powerful weapons and fly.
